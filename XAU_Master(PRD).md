This Product Requirements Document (PRD) outlines the development of "XAU_Master_2026," a dual-purpose system comprising a high-fidelity Visual Indicator for manual confirmation and a fully automated Expert Advisor (EA) for MetaTrader 5 (MT5).1. Project OverviewProject Name: XAU_Master_2026 (Institutional Gold Scalper)Asset: Gold (XAUUSD / GOLD)Timeframe: M1 (Execution) and M5 (Setup)Goal: Capitalize on $30–$100$ point moves using institutional liquidity sweeps and momentum alignment.2. System ArchitectureThe system is divided into two modules that share the same underlying logic:The Indicator Module: Provides on-chart visual cues (Arrows, VWAP, EMA Clouds) and push notifications.The EA Module: Executes trades based on the indicator signals, managing risk and exits autonomously.3. Core Strategy Logic (The "Golden Setup")The system only triggers a trade when three "locks" are engaged:Lock 1: Institutional Bias (M5)EMA Cloud: EMA 20 must be above EMA 50 for Buys; below for Sells.VWAP Filter: Price must be above Daily VWAP for Buys; below for Sells.Psychological Levels: Detection of $5.00$ and $10.00$ round numbers (e.g., $5,350.00$, $5,355.00$).Lock 2: Liquidity Sweep (Price Action)The "Stop Hunt" Detector: Identify when a candle wicks below a previous 30-candle low (for Longs) or above a 30-candle high (for Shorts) and then closes back inside the range.Lock 3: Momentum Trigger (M1)Candle Confirmation: A bullish engulfing or high-volume "pin bar" following the Liquidity Sweep.RSI Hook: RSI (14) must be crossing back above 50 (for Longs) or below 50 (for Shorts).4.
